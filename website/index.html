about me

Hi, I am Gabriel Gomes, a Software Engineer.
I'll be a Future Leader in Digital Sovereignity.

I grew up in a favela, where opportunities for education were scarce, but I was fortunate enough to have access to great opportunities that I could not afford to waste — and I didn’t. Since I was young, using tools like Hamachi and proxies to play with friends, I learned about networks, NAT, and subnets. My curiosity led me to explore modding games, understanding versioning, binaries, and the difference between compiled and non-compiled code. This early exposure showed me that technology was my path, and despite briefly exploring other fields, I am certain my calling is to develop digital solutions that promote my country’s sovereignty. Today, my goal is to create solutions that reduce dependency on big techs, protect our data, and combat digital colonialism, ensuring that our digital resources stay within our control and benefit.
